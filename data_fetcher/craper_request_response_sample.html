Rare converted condo in the heart of the city with 15-foot ceiling heights, exposed concrete walls, quintessential skyline views and an open layout at the prestigious 74 New Montgomery, once a printing press run by the Hearst Family. Unit #608 is a 1 bedroom with den, currently shown as a two-bedroom, 1 bathroom, featuring 952sqft, 15ft ceiling height, exposed concrete wall, large operable windows, city skyline views, light oak floors and high-end kitchen with stainless steel appliances. Building amenities incl. 24hr security, Dry-cleaning service, onsite management and a panoramic 5,500SF+/- roof deck with bbq/wifi/heater. Located next to The Palace Hotel and nearby BART MUNI world reknown museums, restaurants and shopping.
